Recipe #1:Classic Beshbarmak Recipe:

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 5
Ingredients Quantity
lamb or beef 2 lbs
large flat noodles 1 lb
onions, sliced 2
potatoes, diced (optional) 2
cloves garlic, minced (optional) 2

Step 1: Boil the meat until tender in a large pot. Remove the meat and reserve the broth.
Step 2:Cook the noodles in the broth, adding more water if necessary. In the last few minutes, add carrots and green beans to the pot.
Step 3:Sauté onions until golden brown. Add minced garlic for additional flavor.
Step 4:Place the cooked noodles on a serving platter, arrange the boiled meat, and top with sautéed vegetables.
Step 5:Season with salt and black pepper. Serve hot with additional broth on the side.
